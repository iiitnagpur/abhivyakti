ABOUT US

Come join us!!!

Come this Winter the gates of Central India's Largest Cultural Festival are opened. The Cultural Extravaganza, studded with electrifying and exhaling events and competitions. Conceptualized back in 2016, ABHIVYAKTI has come in all realms both qualitatively and quantititavely and has firmly ensourced itself as the centerpiece and the cultural spectrum of the region. Abhivyakti is a Cultural event completely organized and co-ordinated by willingness and true spirits of IIITians and their individual skill and strength are combined with teamwork, in order to produce meaningfull results for the Institute. So come join us all in the event which will held on 10th-11th March 2018.

KAVYANJALI

Kavyanjali is the poetry competition of the annual cultural fest of IIIT Nagpur, Abhivyakti.

Poetry is an art form in which human language is used for its aesthetic qualities in addition to, or instead of, its notional and semantic content. It consists largely of oral or literary works in which language is used in a manner that is felt by its user and audience to differ from ordinary prose.

It may use condensed or compressed form to convey emotion or ideas to the reader's or listener's mind or ear; it may also use devices such as assonance and repetition to achieve musical or incantatory effects. Poems frequently rely for their effect on imagery, word association, and the musical qualities of the language used. The interactive layering of all these effects to generate meaning is what marks poetry.
